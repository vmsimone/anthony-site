About Me

My name is Anthony Simone. I am very passionate about boxing and fitness. I have a degree in Exercise Science and I am a NASM Certified Trainer. I started boxing back in my home town of Phoenix, Arizona and now I fight out of Hollywood, California. I enjoy being a personal trainer helping people reach their goals whether it is losing weight, getting comfortable with a work out routine, learning to box or just staying in shape. I have the expertise to help.
